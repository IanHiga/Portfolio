Lost in Southwest Hall

Unity Project

Group Project

2 Weeks

Play WebGL: https://comp159.github.io/2d-team-game-with-3d-objects-git-and-run/


You manage to lock yourself outside of your dorm room in the infamously haunted Southwest Hall! The RA on duty can let you back in... but what was their phone number again? If yo uwander the halls, maybe the phone number is posted somewhere...

Search the three floors for pieces of the RA's phone number. Be sure to avoid the ghosts that wander the halls at night! Hide in unlocked rooms, wind and place a music box to keep ghosts in place, and use salt to disperse them in a pinch!

This project was created with Unity.

Some notable features:
- Multiple enemies that track the player
- Randomized movment to make ghosts wander and not just chase players
- Hide in unlocked doors
- Find the key to unlock more hiding spots
- Wind up and place a music box to keep ghosts in one spot
- Find and place salt to teleport ghosts away
