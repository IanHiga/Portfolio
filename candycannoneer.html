Candy Cannoneer

Unity Project

Solo Project

1 Week

Itch.io Page: https://trashplus.itch.io/candy-cannoneer


It's Halloween night. The children want candy and they want it now! Keep them at bay with your trusty candy cannon and you might make it through this year's candy mob in one piece.

Enemies spawn in randomly and make their way towards your position. Shoot candy at them to satiate their hunger! You also must occasionally reload your cannon, shaking a new bag of candy into the barrel. As your score grows, the spawn rate increases.

This project was created with Unity for the 2023 Scream Jam.

Some notable features:
- Ramping difficulty over time
- An active reload system
- Randomized Nav Agent movement
- Randomly spawned enemies
- Small story-related elements
