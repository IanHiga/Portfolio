Fire Rescue

Unity Project

Solo Project

1 Week

GitHub: https://github.com/IanHiga/Individual-3D-Project


A fire has started in the city! Use a trampoline to bounce citizens into the ambulance. Make sure to not drop anyone!

Move the trampoline between five positions to bounce citizens. Citizens will bounce to a random location.

This project was created with Unity.

Some notable features:
- Random citizen spawning
- Citizens choose a new location after bouncing at random
- Gain points for bounces and for getting citizens into the ambulance
