Commuter Chaos

Unity Project

Group Project

2 Months

GitHub: https://github.com/IanHiga/Commuter-Chaos


Drive around the city and complete quests. Push through traffic, navigate tight corners, fly over jumps, and crash through a pyramid of buses!

Drive a vehicle through the city and perform quests throughout the map. Quests must be completed one at a time.

This project was created with Unity.

Features I Created:
- Vehicle movement / controller
- Scalable quest and waypoint system
- Reset button
- Ability to cancel quests
