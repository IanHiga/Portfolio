Night Terror

Unity Project

Group Project

2 Weeks

Itch.io Page: https://screamsicles.itch.io/night-terror


After waking up you begin to realize a few things are off about your home. There are no windows, hallways seem to loop into themselves, and a faint sound of footsteps can be heard from somewhere behind the walls. There has to be some way out of this nightmare!

Traverse procedurally generated hallways and rooms to find the way out of this nightmare! Keep an eye out, if the entities in the halls catch you, you may never wake up again!

This project was created with Unity for the 2021 Scream Jam.

Some notable features:
- Procedurally generated hallways
- Non-euclidean geometry
- Occasional story-related rooms
- Randomly spawned enemies
- Small sequenced story events
