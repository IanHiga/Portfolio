Wall Slider

Unity Project

Solo Project

1 Week

GitHub: https://github.com/IanHiga/COMP-159-2D-Game-Project


Slide down the walls for as long as you can!

Jump back and forth between the two walls as long as possible. Collect randomly spawning coins and avoid spikes along the way.

This project was created with Unity.

Notable Features:
- Randomly generated levels
- Spikes and coins are implemented in prefab layouts that spawn specific arrangements randomly
- High scores are locally saved
