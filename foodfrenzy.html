Food Frenzy

Unity Project

Group Project

3 Weeks

GitHub: https://github.com/comp159/final-project-3d-gamer-games-games


Fufill orders for hungry customers in this hectic multitasking kitchen game! Run from station to station to gather and prep ingredients to complete customer requests! Orders will speed up as you do well. Don't miss three in a row or the customers will leave! How many orders can you complete?

Walk between different prep stations to prepare ingredients. Complete orders that file in from hungry customers.

This project was created with Unity.

Features I Created:
- Potato Gathering Minigame
- Soda Filling Minigame
- Randomized orders
- Orders queue and expire over time
- Orders check for and remove ingredients from inventory
- Menus pop up when interacting with stations
