Zombeez: Overrun

Unity Project

Solo Project

5 Days

Itch.io Page: https://trashplus.itch.io/zombeez-overrun


A small Vampire Survivors-inspired, wave-based shooting game. Navigate tight packs of zombies and upgrade your weapons to avoid being eaten alive! Can you defeat the boss and make it out in one piece?

Avoid a horde of zombies and earn coins to upgrade your weapons!

This project was created with Unity for the 2024 Zombeez Game Jam.

The Zombeez Game Jam supplied developers with art assets and sounds to build a prototype with.

Some notable features:
- Ramping difficulty over time
- 5 enemy types
- 3 weapon types
- 5 waves of enemies
- A boss battle with 3 different attack patterns
